About M‑Tek Fire & Safety Ltd

Incorporated on 5th December 2012 (RC: 1082534), M‑Tek Fire & Safety Ltd provides fire safety, security and solar products and services across Nigeria.

We help organisations and individuals protect lives and property through reliable, standard‑compliant safety solutions. From extinguishers and alarm systems to security devices and solar installations, we combine quality products with professional service.

Our team supports offices, schools, hospitals, factories, warehouses, fuel stations and residential estates with tailored safety and power solutions.

Fire, Safety, Security & Solar Equipment
