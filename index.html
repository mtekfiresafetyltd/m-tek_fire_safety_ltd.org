Fire Extinguisher Sales, Installation & Refilling

M-Tek Fire & Safety Ltd provides certified fire extinguisher sales, professional installation, refilling, and maintenance services across Nigeria. We supply CO₂, DCP, Foam, and Water extinguishers suitable for residential, commercial, and industrial environments.

Our technicians ensure correct extinguisher selection, strategic placement, pressure testing, and compliance with safety regulations. We also offer routine inspection and refilling services to keep your equipment operational at all times.

Industries served include offices, schools, hospitals, factories, warehouses, and fuel stations. Contact M-Tek Fire & Safety Ltd for dependable fire extinguisher solutions that protect lives and assets.

Fire Safety Training, Consultancy & Risk Assessment

M-Tek Fire & Safety Ltd delivers professional fire safety training and consultancy services for organizations and individuals. Our programs are designed to improve emergency preparedness, regulatory compliance, and workplace safety culture.

We provide fire risk assessments, evacuation planning, extinguisher handling training, and safety audits tailored to your facility. Our consultants work closely with management teams to identify hazards and implement effective fire prevention strategies.

Training sessions are suitable for corporate organizations, schools, hospitals, construction sites, and industrial facilities. Choose M-Tek Fire & Safety Ltd for expert fire safety guidance and practical training solutions.
